Nicholas Hayek

is an undergraduate at McGill University in beautiful Montreal, Quebec. My spare time is spent generously at the piano, often at a blackboard, and occasionally in service to my university's newspaper. You can find me reading one of Ian McEwan's novels near the fountain in St. Louis Square, typing up my math homework, cursing the plight of the Cleveland Browns, or buried somewhere in the basement of a brutalist building.
